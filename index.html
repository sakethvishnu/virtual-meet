Virtual Call

One of the finest and most interesting piece of technology in your hands!

Virtual call is destination to have the best and most structured 3d live calling which lets you feel a live surrounding that sits right next to you!

It lets you have a projection of the other recipient of your call in your surroundings and it feels as if they are talking as if they are sitting next to you.

Features

HARDWARE DEVICE

This device is the pillar for this idea. This is a touch screen device which has a camera inside it that lets you project the video of the recipient. The in-built microphone also is of great quality and a speaker is used inside for outer sound compatible with the video.

IN-APP DEVELOPMENT

The working of this application/software has its own environment. The user has to host a room or join an already existing room in order to have a live video call. The set of codes are designed in such a way that , the server takes no time in delay and has a very clear passage for connectivity.

WORKING

A Projection of the user is pre-saved and tracked using motion sensors to capture the movement perfectly. A projection of the user is displayed using the device and using portable mini cameras it can be projected at any desired location.
